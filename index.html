Our Mission

At Second Skin Care, we aim to transform the lifecycle of cosmetic ingredients by repurposing expired makeup into innovative leather care solutions.

Through precise extraction and formulation techniques, we are working towards creating premium leather conditioners, color protectants, and hydrating balms that blend science with sustainability.

Our Story

We launched Second Skin Care during an Innovation Sprint—a week-long challenge where industry leaders like L’Oréal and Circular Republic exposed a harsh reality: in Germany, 3 to 5 million units of expired and damaged makeup were being burned each year. That pivotal moment ignited our passion. As co-founders, we, Anna Radová and Naima Lange, realized that waste could be transformed into something valuable. Our journey is driven by the vision to reinvent beauty and sustainability—one breakthrough at a time.
